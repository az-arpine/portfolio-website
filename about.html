About

Get to Know Me

As a classical violinist, I appreciate form, rhythm, and precision — and in design, I strive to apply the same energy to pixels instead of notes.

My musical journey began in Armenia, where I’m originally from, and took me to Switzerland, where I spent two years and earned my MA degree. Eventually, I found my base in Massachusetts, where I currently live.

As a musician, I carry a broad cultural background and have learned to adapt to new environments with confidence and curiosity. I’m detail-oriented and aim to perfect the task at hand. Through performing in orchestras and chamber groups, I’ve learned to function as one unit with a team — while still giving my all and bringing my personality into the process. During my time at CareerFoundry, I learned to center my projects around the user and their needs. By utilizing tools like Figma, Microsoft Office, and others, I’ve built a strong foundation in user research, wireframing, and designing both native and responsive web applications. I’m a fast learner and look forward to being part of a team where ideas bounce and build.

On a beautiful day, I love spending time outdoors with my husband and two active children. You can also find me in my backyard — either visualizing my next project or tending to my garden and flower beds.
